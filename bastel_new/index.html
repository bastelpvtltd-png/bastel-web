Who We Are

Bastel Pvt Ltd — Your Trusted Partner

Incorporated under the laws of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, Bastel Pvt Ltd is a full-service trade and logistics company headquartered in Colombo. We exist to make international trade seamless for Sri Lankan businesses and global partners alike.

From customs house agency and freight handling to fumigation and final documentation, our expert team manages every link in your supply chain — so you can focus on growing your business.

🎯

Mission

To deliver reliable, compliant, and cost-effective trade solutions that empower businesses to compete on the global stage.

🌐

Vision

To be Sri Lanka's most trusted global trade catalyst — renowned for integrity, expertise, and seamless execution.

⚡

Core Values

Integrity · Precision · Partnership · Compliance · Innovation — the five pillars that guide every decision we make.

How It Works

Our End-to-End
Trade Process

01

Initial Consultation

We assess your cargo, destination, and regulatory requirements to design the optimal shipping route and documentation plan.

02

Freight Booking

Secure bookings with our trusted airline and shipping line partners — air or sea, FCL or LCL, we get your cargo moving.

03

Customs Clearance

Our licensed agents handle all customs declarations, duty assessments, and regulatory checks for smooth port clearance.

04

Fumigation & Compliance

Where required, certified fumigation treatments are carried out to meet phytosanitary and biosecurity standards.

05

Transport to Destination

From port to warehouse or factory floor — our fleet ensures your cargo arrives safely and on schedule.

06

Final Documentation

Complete issuance of all trade documents — BL, COO, certificates, invoices — neatly compiled and delivered.
